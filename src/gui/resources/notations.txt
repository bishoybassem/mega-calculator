Notations : 

Operations :
          Addition  =  +
          Subtraction  =  -
          Multiplication  =  *
          Division  =  /
          Power  =  ^
          Remainder  =  %

Functions :
          Natural logarithm  =  ln
          Common logarithm  =  log
          Square root  =  cbrt
          Cubic root  =  sqrt
          Factorial  =  fac
          Absolute  =  abs
          Trig. functions  =  sin , cos , tan , sec , csc , cot
          Inverse trig. functions = arcsin , arccos , arctan , arcsec , arccsc , arccot
          Hyperbolic functions = sinh , cosh , tanh , sech , csch , coth
          Inverse hyperbolic functions = arcsinh , arccosh , arctanh , arcsech , arccsch , arccoth
	
Constants :
          pi ,  e
        
Notes :
        - You can use parentheses to group terms
        - Spaces are ignored
          Ex : s   i n(3  0) = sin(30)
          
Function Examples :
          F(x) = (2x^3 + x^2) / 2
          F(x) = sin(x)^2  + cos(x)^2
          F(x) = 2ln(x) + 3log(x) + 4e^x + 2
          F(x) = sqrt(x^2 + 4) + abs(x - 3)
